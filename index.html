The Artist

ZHU Li Juan was born in 1967 to a modest working-class family in Chengdu, Sichuan. Growing up during the height of China’s infamous Cultural Revolution, Lijuan learned at a young age to always observe others quietly and to keep her thoughts to herself. It was during this oppressive time that Lijuan learned to express her feelings through drawing and painting. Her family quickly realised that she had a natural talent in art, and despite their humble background did everything they could to encourage and cultivate her talent.

Her Artwork

Over the years, Li Juan has moved between Zurich, Como, Hong Kong, and Chengdu in search of subjects and motivation for her paintings. With a natural flare for colour and years of training, Lijuan’s art work effortlessly combines Western impressionism with Eastern art techniques to create pieces of great beauty and serenity.
